1
What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your browser or device. They help websites and apps remember information about your visit — such as your session, preferences, or language setting.

We also use similar technologies including local storage and session tokens. All references to "cookies" in this policy include these similar technologies.

The Arivo guest app is a web application accessed via a browser. As such, it uses cookies governed by the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and the EU ePrivacy Directive, in addition to UK/EU GDPR.

Cookie Categories We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies
No consent required — these are essential for the app to function

Your Cookie Choices

When you first use the Arivo app or admin panel, you will be shown a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept all cookies, accept only essential cookies, or customise your preferences.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of the app.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling all cookies may affect the functionality of the app. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

For mobile browsers, cookie controls are typically found in your device's browser settings or the browser app's settings menu.

No Advertising or Tracking

Arivo does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any technology designed to profile you for marketing purposes. We do not participate in any advertising networks.

The Arivo platform and guest app are ad-free by design.
