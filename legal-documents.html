Luvia uses cookies and similar technologies in both our hotel admin dashboard and our guest-facing app. This policy explains what we use, why, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website or app. They help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how our platform is being used. Some cookies are essential for the platform to function; others are optional.

Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the platform to work. They enable hotel login sessions, security tokens, and basic functionality. They cannot be disabled.
Always Active
Examples: arivo_session (authentication), csrf_token (security), arivo_hotel_id (identify your property)
Functional Cookies
These remember your preferences — such as your chosen language, dashboard layout, and notification settings — so you don't have to reset them every visit.
Examples: arivo_prefs (dashboard settings), arivo_lang (language preference)
Analytics Cookies
These help us understand how hotels and guests use the platform — which features are most used, where people drop off, and how to improve. Data is anonymised and aggregated. We use Plausible Analytics (privacy-friendly, no cross-site tracking).
Examples: plausible_session (anonymous usage tracking)
Marketing Cookies
We currently do not use marketing or advertising cookies on our platform. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and seek your consent before placing any marketing cookies.
Not used

Cookies in the Guest App

The guest-facing app uses minimal cookies. Because the app is accessed via a QR code without a login, we use only a session token to maintain continuity during a guest's visit to the app. No persistent tracking cookies are placed on guest devices. The session cookie expires when the browser is closed.

Third-Party Cookies

Where we embed third-party functionality — such as WhatsApp links — those third parties may set their own cookies subject to their own privacy policies. We do not control third-party cookies. No advertising networks have access to your data through Luvia.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent the hotel dashboard from functioning. Guidance for major browsers:

  • Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox — Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Updates to This Policy

We will update this Cookie Policy whenever we add or remove cookies from our platform. The latest version is always available at luviaguest.com/cookies.
